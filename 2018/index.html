What is TechnoHacks?

TechnoHacks is Skyline High School's upcoming weekend-long invention marathon designed to provide students an opportunity to create impactful projects, learn coding, and win prizes.

This year's theme is sustainability. Students will tackle the United Nation's 17 Sustainability Goals to build solutions for a better world.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is where you turn your crazy ideas into real stuff. You'll learn how to design or code an app and pitch your idea to judges!

How much does it cost?

TechnoHacks is completely free! Snacks and caffeine will be provided. Meals are not included, so plan accordingly.

Who can attend?

Open to all Issaquah and Lake Washington School District students aged 13 and up. Workshops will guide newcomers in ideation and coding.

Why focus on sustainability?

Inspired by Technovation, TechnoHacks encourages projects that align with the UN's 17 Sustainability Goals, fostering innovation for social good.
